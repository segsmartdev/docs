Follow these steps to configure your white label platform:

## 1. Activate White Label

1. Purchase the white label plan
2. Wait for activation confirmation
3. Access your dashboard

## 2. Access White Label Panel

1. Look for the "White Label Panel" button in your dashboard
   <Frame>
     <img
       src="/resources/white-label-button.jpg"
       alt="White Label Panel Button Location"
     />
   </Frame>
2. Click to access white label settings

## 3. Configure Platform Settings

In the White Label Panel, you can customize:

- Platform name
- Brand colors
- Logo
- Domain settings
- Email templates
- Support contact information

<Frame>
  <img src="/resources/wl-1.jpg" alt="White Label Settings Page" />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img src="/resources/wl-2.jpg" alt="White Label Settings Page" />
</Frame>

## 4. Set Up Billing

1. Click your user profile
2. Select "Billing platform"
   <Frame>
     <img src="/resources/stripe-button.jpg" alt="Billing Dashboard Access" />
   </Frame>
3. Connect your Stripe account to start collecting payments
4. Configure payment settings

## 5. Domain Configuration

1. Choose your domain (e.g., app.yourbrand.com)
2. Add the provided DNS records to your domain
3. Wait for SSL certificate provisioning
4. Test domain access

## 6. Testing

Before launch:

1. Create a test account
2. Verify all branding elements
3. Test the signup process
4. Make test calls
5. Verify billing flow
